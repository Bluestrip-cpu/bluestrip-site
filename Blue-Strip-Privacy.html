Privacy Policy

We keep things simple — here's exactly what we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Blue Strip Hospitality ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses information when you visit our website or contact us for a quote. We keep this simple because we collect very little.

Information we collect

When you submit our quote or contact form, or message us, we collect only what you choose to share, typically:

  • Your name and the property/business name
  • Your phone number and/or email address
  • Details of your enquiry (products, quantities, requirements)

We do not knowingly collect any sensitive personal data, and we do not require you to create an account.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and prepare a quote
  • To arrange samples, orders and delivery
  • To contact you about your order or related questions

We do not sell, rent or share your information with third parties for marketing.

How we store it

Enquiries reach us by email and messaging, and are stored only as long as needed to serve you and keep normal business records. We take reasonable measures to keep your information secure.

Cookies & analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand visitor numbers and improve the site. These do not identify you personally. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices

You may ask us to confirm what information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. Just contact us using the details below.

Contact

For any privacy question, email info@bluestrip.in or call +91 90510 00070.

This policy is provided as a general template for an informational, quote-based business website. Before going live, please have it reviewed against the data-protection laws that apply to your business (in India, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act) to ensure it fully fits your operations.
