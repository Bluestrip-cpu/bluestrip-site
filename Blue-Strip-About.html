Our story

Built in Panipat, trusted across India.

Blue Strip Hospitality began in 2014 with a simple conviction: hotels deserve linen that performs as hard as they do, supplied by the people who actually make it — not passed through a chain of traders who add cost at every step.

From our manufacturing base in Panipat, Haryana — India's textile heartland — we source quality fabric and cut, stitch and finish bedding, bath and room linen under one roof. Over a decade on, that control is what lets us promise consistent quality, dependable supply and fair, manufacturer-direct pricing.

Today more than 10,000 properties — from boutique guest houses to large resorts — rely on us, order after order.
